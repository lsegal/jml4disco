1. Milestone 1

1.1 Risk Identification

1.1.1 Technology Risks

  • The project entails work using JML4, Distributed Systems and Boogie, all of which are technologies all but one team member have worked with before.

1.1.2 People Risks

  • It is impossible to recruit staff familiar with the technologies described above.

1.1.3 Organizational Risks

  • Management roles will change as the project continues.
  • The requirements are as of yet still unclear to most of the team.
  • Very open scope presents the threat of 'feature creep'.
